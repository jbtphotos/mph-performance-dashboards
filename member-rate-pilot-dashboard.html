What This Dashboard Shows

This analysis compares rate plan booking behavior in the prior year (before the member rate existed for legacy properties) vs the pilot period (Nov 13, 2025 – present). All rate plans are grouped into 7 categories. The PMS and Website channels are analyzed independently to show where Member Rate bookings are coming from and whether guests are trading down from Nightly or other higher-priced rate plans.
