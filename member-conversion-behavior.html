Narrative 1
Net-New Guest Acquisition
93.1%
The vast majority of Stay Rewarded members are brand-new to My Place Hotels. Only 226 of 3,280 members had any prior booking history — meaning the program is driving genuine new business, not just rewarding existing guests.
Narrative 2
OTA → Direct Channel Shift
48% → 0%
Before joining, 48.2% of returning-guest bookings came through OTA/GDS channels (Expedia, Booking.com, etc.). After converting to Stay Rewarded, 100% of their bookings are direct — eliminating OTA commissions entirely.
Narrative 3
Members Are Not Trading Down
+15.7%
Prior stays averaged $95.05 ADR. After becoming members, the same pool averages $109.97 ADR — a $14.92 increase. The member rate program is not cannibalizing revenue; it's growing it while building loyalty.
