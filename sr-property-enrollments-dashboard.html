Two Enrollment Streams

This dashboard tracks two distinct enrollment sources for the Stay Rewarded program across 32 participating properties. Property Enrollments (18,167) are guests signed up at check-in or converted from OTA bookings at the front desk. Member Rate Program Enrollments (3,341) are guests who enrolled through the member rate booking flow — either via PMS at the property or on the website. Together they represent 21,508 total enrollments. The Non-Legacy cohort (7 properties) has been live since June 9, 2025; the Legacy cohort (25 properties) launched November 13, 2025.

Key Takeaway

Property-level enrollments account for 84.5% of all Stay Rewarded sign-ups, demonstrating the critical role front desk staff and OTA conversion play in program growth. The Member Rate program adds an additional 3,341 enrollments (15.5%) through the booking flow. Non-Legacy properties average 1,071 combined enrollments per property vs 560 for Legacy, though Legacy has had less than half the time in-program.
